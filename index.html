HIR:Health Information Resource

HealthHub is your trusted resource for in-depth information on common diseases, offering detailed descriptions, symptom guides, preventive measures, and recommended medications to support informed health management.

  • Influenza (Flu)
  • Common cold
  • Hypertension

Influenza or Flu

Description: Overview of influenza, its causes, and how it spreads.

Symptoms: Common symptoms such as fever, cough, and body aches.

Precautions: Tips on flu prevention, including vaccination and hygiene.

Medications: Information on antiviral medications and over-the-counter remedies.

Common Cold

Description: Basic information on the common cold and its viral origins.

Symptoms: Listing of cold symptoms like runny nose and sore throat.

Precautions: Hygiene practices to prevent the spread of the cold.

Medications: Over-the-counter remedies for symptom relief.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Description: Explanation of hypertension, its risk factors, and effects on health.

Symptoms: Information on signs like headaches and dizziness.

Precautions: Lifestyle changes to manage blood pressure, including diet and exercise.

Medications: Details on prescription medications and their effects.

Ashtma

Description: Overview of asthma, its causes, and the impact on the airways.

Symptoms: Common asthma symptoms, including shortness of breath and wheezing.

Precautions: Asthma management tips, including avoiding triggers and using inhalers.

Medications: Information on asthma medications, both quick-relief and controller medications.

Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye)

Description: Overview of conjunctivitis, an inflammation of the thin, clear tissue covering the white part of the eye and the inside of the eyelids.

Symptoms: Common symptoms of pink eye, including redness, itchiness, tearing, and discharge.

Precautions: Hygiene practices to prevent the spread of conjunctivitis, such as avoiding touching the eyes and frequent handwashing.

Medications: Information on treatment options, including over-the-counter or prescription eye drops and ointments for relief.
